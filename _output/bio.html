Michael A. Dorato PhD, DABT, ATS

  • Executive Vice President
  • Smithers Avanza Toxicology Services

Dr. Dorato has served as Vice-President and CSO, Global Discovery Services (Covance) and Executive Director, Toxicology (Eli Lilly and Co.). In these positions he led global organizations in safety and efficacy lead optimization, investigative and regulatory toxicology, discovery and late phase pathology, animal welfare, molecular and anatomical imaging, in vivo pharmacology (oncology, neuroscience, cardiovascular and metabolic disease, inflammation, renal, bone, receptor occupancy), veterinary medicine and model development. He was the Institutional Official in both a major pharmaceutical company and a CRO. He was a member of the Discovery Executive Team for Eli Lilly and Company, and as a principal member of the Lilly Committee responsible for the transfer of the Greenfield Laboratories to Covance. He led the integration of the former Lilly staff in to the Covance organization. He has held adjunct professorships at Indiana University, Butler University, St. John’s University, and am currently a Visiting Scholar at St. John’s University College of Pharmacy.
